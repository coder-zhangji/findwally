Find Wally

Ji Zhang && Zhuoqun Wang





Where’s Waldo (or Wally) is a series of children’s books that challenges the reader with finding the eponymous character and his compadres in densely illustrated images.

The web application can help users upload their images containing wally. Once you upload successfully, system will label wally in the images using a box along with the prediction's accuracy. The whole process may take 30-40 seconds.
